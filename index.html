About

Sub-Earth is a first-person virtual reality game for HTC Vive, set in a futuristic colony at the bottom of the Pacific Ocean. Players will navigate an immersive underwater environment, observing their surroundings in order to solve problems and make it safely back to the Sub-Earth Technologies base camp. Sub-Earth will make full use of virtual reality technology to create a believable underwater adventure experience unlike any game currently on the market.
